Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products in original condition with packaging. Electronics must be unopened or show no signs of use. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! We provide free technical support for all products purchased from AngelTech. You can reach our support team by phone, email, or live chat during business hours. For complex issues, we offer remote desktop support.

What shipping options do you offer?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (1-2 business days), and overnight shipping. Shipping is free on orders over $500. International shipping is available to select countries with varying delivery times.

Do you price match competitors?

Yes, we offer a 30-day price match guarantee. If you find the same product at a lower price from an authorized retailer, we'll match it. Contact our customer service with the competitor's offer details to initiate the price match.

What warranty do your products come with?

All products come with at least a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Many electronics have extended warranties available for purchase. Warranty details are included with each product and can also be found on our website.
